EXECUTIVE TOOL | TOWN HALL TALKING POINTS
LEADERSHIP TOOLKIT
Town Hall Talking Points
Quarterly All-Hands: Transformation Progress Update
⏱ Duration: 60 minutes | Format: Virtual with Q&A | Audience: All professionals
Opening (5 minutes)

Hook: "Six months ago, we committed to transforming how we work. Today, I want to share the remarkable progress we've made together and where we're heading next."

Acknowledge: Recognize the extra effort during this transition. Name specific teams or individuals who've gone above and beyond.

Frame: Position this as a journey we're on together, not a top-down mandate.

Progress Update (15 minutes)

Win Stories (humanize the data)

  • "The Chicago team used our new approach to win a $15M engagement in just 3 weeks—previously would have taken 6 weeks"
  • "Sarah in Toronto said the new tools gave her 5 hours back each week to focus on client relationships"
  • "Our client NPS increased 12 points—clients specifically mentioned our increased agility"

By the Numbers (keep it simple)

  • 1,500 professionals trained (85% of target)
  • 30% reduction in proposal development time
  • $50M in new pursuits using enhanced methodology
Addressing Concerns (10 minutes)

Acknowledge challenges directly:

"I know many of you are feeling the pressure of learning new systems while maintaining client delivery. This is normal and temporary."

Provide specific support:

  • "We've added 20 transformation coaches available for 1:1 support"
  • "Office hours expanded to cover all time zones"
  • "Simplified quick reference guides based on your feedback"
Q&A Preparation

Anticipated Questions & Key Messages:

Q: "When will things return to normal?"
A: "This IS our new normal—but the intensive learning phase ends in Q2. By then, these new ways of working will feel natural."

Q: "How does this affect promotions/compensation?"
A: "Performance expectations account for transformation efforts. We're measuring adoption alongside traditional metrics."

Q: "What if the technology doesn't work as promised?"
A: "We have contingency plans and are monitoring closely. Report issues immediately—we fix what's not working."

Closing (5 minutes)

Reinforce the vision: "We're not just changing processes—we're positioning ourselves to lead the profession for the next decade."

Call to action: "I need each of you to: 1) Complete your training by month-end, 2) Share feedback openly, 3) Support your colleagues through this transition."

Express confidence: "I've never been more confident in our team's ability to achieve something extraordinary together."
